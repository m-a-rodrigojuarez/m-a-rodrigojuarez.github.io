About me

Researcher, technologist, teacher, and constant learner.

Hi, I'm Rodrigo, a Senior UX Researcher based in Ensenada, Baja California. My work brings together computer science, software development, research, product strategy, and a practical understanding of how operations work in the real world.

I studied Computer Engineering at the Instituto Tecnologico Autonomo de Mexico (ITAM) and later earned a Master's degree in Computer Science from the Centro de Investigacion Cientifica y de Educacion Superior de Ensenada (CICESE).

Most recently, I worked at Walmart MX, where I started in Research & UX Ops and later moved into a UX Research Manager role. During that time, I supported the eCommerce team through the final stage of a major migration to a unified technology platform across the website, mobile apps, and operations. After that, I joined OmniSupply Transportation, where I led UX researches for first-, middle-, and last-mile platforms used by customers and internal teams, including warehouse workers, carriers, and administrative staff.

I currently work as a part-time lecturer at Universidad Icesi, where I teach a Master's course on Digital Product Evaluation Methods. I have also worked with UX research agencies and in software development for Hoteles City Express.

Outside of work, I love football and have practiced CrossFit for the past 14 years. Both have taught me a lot about consistency, preparation, teamwork, and the discipline it takes to keep growing into a better version of myself.

Research Approach

Behavior-driven product research

I design and lead mixed-method research initiatives that connect user behavior, operational realities, and business strategy to improve product decisions and drive measurable outcomes.

My work focuses on identifying friction across critical journeys, understanding behavioral patterns, and translating insights into scalable product opportunities.

Product activation

Understanding onboarding friction, user behavior, and critical moments across digital journeys to improve activation and time-to-value.

Strategic scalability

Connecting operational context, market variability, and business priorities to support scalable product and service decisions.

Behavioral optimization

Using behavioral analysis and flow optimization to identify opportunities that improve conversion and business performance.
